excludeItems Plugin

Put this in your Perchance code panel:

excludeItems = \{import:exclude-items-plugin\}


Now you can cut items out of lists:

\[excludeItems(animal, "bear", "lion")\]

That will output a random item from the animal list, excluding "bear" and "lion".


This will randomly select an item from the race list that isn't in the evilRace list:

\[excludeItems(race, evilRace.selectAll)\]


And let's also prevent "elf", "gnome" and "human" from being chosen:

\[excludeItems(race, evilRace.selectAll, "elf", "gnome", "human")\]


It essentially creates a new list so you can do normal stuff to it:

\[excludeItems(plant, "cactus").selectMany(5)\]

\[p = excludeItems(plant, "cactus"), ""\]
